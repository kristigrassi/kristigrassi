I lead the Product Design practice at Pivotal Labs in Los Angeles where I work to transform the way organizations approach product by bringing a human-centered perspective and putting design thinking into practice.

What I do

User Research

I look beyond trends and seek to solve real human problems through exploratory and qualatative user research. I go out into the world and talk to real people before making decisons based on assumptions.

User Experience

Design isn’t just about making things look pretty, it’s about solving problems. The way we interact with products is a journey with a beginning and an end, and when we’re enjoying the journey we stick to it. I focus on the charm and elegance that takes place along the way.

Lean + Agile

I work closely with developers and product managers in short iterative cyles. No more pixel perfect mockups being handed over a fence.

Facilitation

I bring groups together and provide better decision making techniques that move the product forward.
